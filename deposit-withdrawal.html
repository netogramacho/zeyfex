FAQ

What deposit options do you have?

All our deposit methods are shown in the deposit table above.

What is the minimum deposit?

For Standard Account type there is no minimum deposit.

What is My Wallet?

My wallet Is an internal virtual multicurrency wallet where you can deposit/withdraw or transfer funds on your trading accounts.

What currencies can I use to deposit funds into my trading account?

You can deposit in any of the following: EUR, USD, JPY, GBP, BTC, MYR.

How do BTC deposits work?

BTC deposits are converted into USD and added to your wallet.

How do I register a Credit/Debit Card?

Once you are in the Client Portal, click on My Wallet->Your Credit Cards-> Add a Credit Card >Enter Details->Submit. You also need to provide us with colour copies of the front and back of your Credit Cards that you used to deposit with. Please cover the middle 8 digits on the front of the Card and the CVV number (3-digit number) on the back of the card for security purposes.

How do BTC withdrawals work?

Withdrawals will be requested in USD and converted to BTC for processing.

What withdrawal options do you have?

You can find withdrawal option in the withdrawal section in the table above.

Do you charge any withdrawal fees?

You can find withdrawal fees in the withdrawal section in the table above.

How long does it take to process my withdrawal?

Upon request, the withdrawal procedure takes up to 24 hours to be processed.

Can I withdraw using a different method than the one I used for my deposit?

Your withdrawals must be transferred to the same card, e-wallet or bank account used for your deposit.

How can I transfer funds between my accounts?

Once you are in the Client Portal, click on My Wallet->Internal Transfer->Enter Details (Wallet to Trading Account or Trading account to Wallet)->Submit Request.
