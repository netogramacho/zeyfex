How to make deposits in the account?

Just log in to your client area, on the left side of the screen, by clicking on "Fund Account". After that, choose the option “deposit”, selecting the trading account where you will make the deposit and clicking “Submit”.

Is it necessary to make a minimum deposit on MT5 accounts?

The minimum deposit for both Standard accounts is from $1 USD.

What are the deposit methods accepted at Zeyfex?

Zeyfex offers cards, bank transfers, Skrill, Neteller, Bitcoin, and if you are from Brazil, you can make the deposit through your bank. For more information, visit the link: deposit & withdraw

What is the fee charged for deposits?

No fee is charged for deposit. You can find the deposit methods at this link: https://zeyfex.com/deposit-withdrawal

How long does it take to make a deposit?

The delay in the deposit will depend on the method you use. Online methods are instantaneous, unlike bank transfers, which can take days. See this link for specific times for each method:https://zeyfex.com/deposit-withdrawal

Yes!

Does Zeyfex accept deposits from third parties?

How to download the trading platform?

The terminal can be downloaded directly from your customer area. https://login.zeyfex.com/downloads

How to access the MT5 platform?

Open your trading platform and click File > Login to the trading account. That done, just enter your account number, your password and choose a relevant server.

What devices can I use to trade?

You can access the software on Windows and Mac Desktop computers. In addition to these, it is also possible to access through the applications available for cell phones and tablets.

Are the values ​​transmitted live without delay?

Yes, all values ​​on MT5 platforms are live, without any delays.

Are there any subscription fees charged on the MT5 platform?

No, MT5 are offered free of charge.

How to change the language?

In the top menu click View> Languages. Select the language you want, close and reopen the platform to update the language.

Does the MT5 platform display the bid or sales amount?

The graphs always show the bid amount. To view the ASK value, right-click on the graph, selecting Properties> Common and check the "Show Ask line" box.

What are the server hours? Can this be changed?

The server's time on the platform is GMT + 2 or GMT + 3 according to daylight saving time. This means that NY closing times can be monitored.

How to install an EA?

Open the platform and click File> Open data folder> MQL5> Experts and paste the .ex4 file. When closing and reopening the platform, the EA will be displayed in the Browser window under “Specialists”.

How do I know which lever I am using?

Just hover your mouse over the account number in the Browser window and you will see your balance and leverage.

How to download a trading statement?

Go to the “Account history” tab, right click and select “Custom period”. Enter the dates you want to receive the statement and press "OK". Then, right-click again and select "Save as report". The Statement will open in an HTML file.

How to add an indicator?

Drag the indicator from the browser window to the chart and it will automatically be pinned to the chart.

How to enter a trade?

Just double-click where you want to trade, directly in the market observation window and a new window with the name “New Order” will appear.

Does the demo account have an expiration date?

If you use your demo account on a regular basis, it will not expire. But if you don't use it for more than 30 days, it will automatically be removed and will not be reactivated again.
